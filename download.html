In order to install this app, you must allow your phone to install 3rd part apps. In order to do this, do the following:

  1. Go to your phone's Settings Menu, and tap the option "Applications"
  2. Look for the option that says "Unknown Sources." Fill the checkbox next to it, then click "OK" to the resulting popup alert.
